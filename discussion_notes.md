# Collaborative Discussion Notes

## Budget Manager Ideas
- **Digital-First Ad Strategy**
  - *Pros*: Measurable, flexible, and scalable. Supports data-driven optimization. 
  - *Cons*: Online ad competition is high, could require rapid budget adjustments and constant monitoring.

- **Local Influencer Partnerships**
  - *Pros*: Cost-effective, can build trust in specific regions, targets community-driven markets.
  - *Cons*: ROI may be harder to track than digital ads; finding authentic influencers takes time.

- **Seasonal Bundle Promotions**
  - *Pros*: Drives short-term sales, leverages gift-buying seasons, high perceived value for customers.
  - *Cons*: May lead to inventory challenges, only effective during promo periods.

## Market Research Expert Ideas
- **Clean Beauty Movement Messaging**
  - *Pros*: Aligns with powerful consumer trends, supports brand reputation, upsells eco-friendly positioning.
  - *Cons*: Must validate claims and packaging, takes effort to ensure authenticity.

- **Social Proof & Testimonials**
  - *Pros*: Boosts product trust, free/organic marketing via user amplification, can go viral.
  - *Cons*: Requires moderation, potential for negative/unverified reviews, needs a robust collection strategy.

- **Competitor Gap Analysis**
  - *Pros*: Highlights strengths, targets premium market, may justify higher prices.
  - *Cons*: Risks direct comparison with competitors, must avoid negative messaging.

## Product Design Expert Ideas
- **Limited Edition Fragrance Line**
  - *Pros*: Creates excitement and urgency, easy to market, freshens up portfolio.
  - *Cons*: Development and inventory costs, uncertain long-term impact.

- **Eco-Friendly Packaging Redesign**
  - *Pros*: Positions Dove as a leader in sustainability, draws in eco-conscious buyers.
  - *Cons*: Initial cost is high, supply chain and regulatory complexity, needs transparent communication.

- **Interactive Store Displays**
  - *Pros*: Increases engagement, memorable POS experience for customers, strong retail differentiation.
  - *Cons*: Not scalable to all retail locations, higher upfront design and installation cost.
